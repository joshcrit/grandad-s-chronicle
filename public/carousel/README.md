# Carousel Photos

Place your 40 carousel photos here.

## Naming Convention

Name them:
- `photo1.jpg`
- `photo2.jpg`
- `photo3.jpg`
- ...
- `photo40.jpg`

## Organization

- Photos 1-10: Row 1 (slides left to right)
- Photos 11-20: Row 2 (slides right to left)
- Photos 21-30: Row 3 (slides left to right)
- Photos 31-40: Row 4 (slides right to left)

## Supported Formats

- JPG/JPEG
- PNG
- WebP

## Tips

- Keep file sizes under 500KB each for faster loading
- Recommended dimensions: 800x800px or similar square format
- The carousel will automatically resize them
